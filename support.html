Support Us 

We need a helping hand: We solicit the cooperation of well-wishers and Donors and welcome contributions in cash and/or kind. Development interventions such as Empowerment of children through Neighbourhood children parliaments, Health Promotion in schools, Youth and Adolescents life skill development, Women Empowerment through livelihood enhancement, women and adolescent girls health, environment and climate change- your donation can make some lives smile

Specific functions of the Apex Body with respect to SHGs are

  • To facilitate the SHGs to audit their group accounts.
  • To facilitate to solve the problems of SHGs if any .
  • To organise grading and credit rating programs for SHGs and refer the eligible groups for bank linkages and credit assistance from Banks and schemes.
  • To plan and implement the MDGs/SDGs related programs with SHGs in the respective villages.

Monitoring and Management of CBOs

  • To build the capacity of the CBOs and member groups.
  • To facilitate linkages with Banks, Government departments, networks and other organizations for the development of the CBOs.
  • To provide need based support guidance and monitoring services.
  • To support in documenting the process and progress of the CBOs.
