OUR STORY

PEOPLES ORGANISATION EDUCATION AND TRAINING SOCIETY (POETS)

POETS, a Secular, Non Profit making Voluntary Organization started in 1986, with a commitment to work in the rural & urban slums areas to help the poor, vulnerable and marginalized people in self- help efforts for their own development. It was formed by Dr. P. Saminathan with the inspiration from Dr. Daleep S. Mukarji, an internationally renowned development expert.
