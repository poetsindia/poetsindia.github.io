About Us

 

 

Governing Board

Governing Board of POETS consists of 15 members of which 8 are Women indicate the gender commitment of the organization.  The General Body meets once in a year to conduct its statutory business and to make policy decisions. The management committee has 9 members and they meet at least 3 times in a year to give guidance to the functional team. The Director is assigned with the responsibility for day to day affairs.
