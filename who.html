Who We Are

 

 

POETS, a Secular, Non Profit making Voluntary Organization started in 1986, with a commitment to work in the rural & urban slums areas to help the poor, vulnerable and marginalized people in self- help efforts for their own development. It was formed by Dr. P. Saminathan with the inspiration from Dr. Daleep S. Mukarji, an internationally renowned development expert. The founder carries with him vast and long years of experience in this sector, since his debut with RUHSA - a Department of Christian Medical College and Hospital, Vellore. POETS is being actively involved in Rural Development Initiatives through Community Based Organizations (CBOs) such as Women SHGs, Federations, Village Task Forces (VTFs), Youth and Adolescents Groups and Children Parliaments. It is also collaborating with Government Departments, PRIs and other networking organization. The primary stakeholders of the programmes are Women, Youth, Adolescents and Children from the marginalized Communities POETS field initiatives were commenced in the year 1987, in 15 villages located at the border of Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh with own funds. The full-fledged activities were undertaken from 1995, with the partnership of international agencies. At present the activities are carried out in the entire Gudiyattam Rural Block (44 panchayats) of Vellore District, Tamil Nadu and in Kurinchipadi block of Cuddalore District.

  • The Board members play dynamic and active role in strengthening POETS programs and linkages.
  • From 2005, Mrs. S. Triveni has become the Director based on her leadership potentials and field experiences in empowerment of women, children and adolescents.
  • POETS Puthiyapaathai - women managing CBO with a mass base of women members extending micro credit support services for livelihoodenhancement and involved in MDGs related programs with networking. It has reached sustainability to a larger extent from 2010 without any external financial support and this CBO is a greater strength for POETS initiatives.
