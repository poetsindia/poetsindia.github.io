CBOs promoted

Puthiya Paathai –meaning New path towards Development.
Puthiya Deepam –meaning New light towards Development.

Puthiya Paathai and Puthiya Deepam are the apex bodies pf Women SHGs federation promoted by POETS in Gudiyattam and Cuddalore Districts respectively with the vision of Building up a Society which does not have socio-economic and Gender disparities wherein people live with Unity and Dignity.

Mission

  • To facilitate SHGs and cluster level federations to become Community Based Organisations involving themselves in Development initiatives.
  • To promote a women bank as an alternative People’s Bank with active participation of women SHGs for sustainable Micro credit services and also enhance the quality life of marginalised and poor in the project areas.
  • To enhance the capacities of women through capacity building Programs and the process of empowerment.

To realise the above mission in reality, a model women bank has been evolved to facilitate Micro Credit Services to its member SHGs with basic concepts such as Credit with dignity, Right to dignity and credit plus. Economic independence and social security for women are the twin integrated inbuilt purposes of these CBOs.

Transparency, Accountability and meaningful participation of stakeholders are the key principles of the CBO to achieve its desired objectives.
The apex bodies (Puthiya Paathai in Gudiyattam and Puthiya Deepam in Cuddalore) has been registered under Tamilnadu Societies Registration Act 27 of 1975 and the Trust Act respectively
